# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2026**

CodeSnap (“the App”) is a barcode scanning utility developed by Fybre (“the Developer”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data. CodeSnap is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

CodeSnap does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- Personal information
- Contact information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers
- Device identifiers
- Scan history or barcode data
- Photos, media, or files

No data is sent to external servers, third parties, or the developer.

---

## Camera Access

The App requires access to your device’s **camera** solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes.

- Camera input is processed **locally on your device**.
- No images or video frames are captured, stored, or transmitted.

You may revoke camera access at any time through your device settings.

---

## Scan Data

All scanned barcode values are processed and stored **locally on your device**.

- Scan history never leaves your device.
- You may clear your history at any time from within the App.

The Developer has no access to your scan data.

---

## Third‑Party Services

The App does **not** use any third‑party analytics, tracking tools, advertising networks, or external SDKs that collect personal data.

---

## Children’s Privacy

CodeSnap does not collect any personal information and is safe for users of all ages. The App does not target children specifically.

---
